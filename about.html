DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE


The Department of Computer Science came to existence in the year 2001 as an unaided self financing department with the objective of imparting quality education and accelerating nation’s economic growth by developing innovative ideas and translating them into commercial products that benefit society. The department has always produced quality professionals, holding important positions in Information Technology industry in India and abroad. Over a decade, it has transformed itself into an aided department that creates an innovative academic environment, with distinctive emphasis on the overall development of students. Besides academic courses and soft skills training, research and innovations are given due importance. Remedial coaching to the weak students has been acknowledeged and appreciated by parents and students alike. The placement record of over 45% to its out going students is an example of what the department is capable of and recruiters of the likes of Tata Consultancy Services, Wipro Technologies, UST Global, Syntel Inc., Eureka Forbes and IBPS offered jobs to its students in the past. Another important initiative, the department has started in the recent past, is the UGC NET coaching targeting students on and off the campus. The department was instrumental in the development of S-Tracker, a software for the easy management of semestrization.The duration of the B.Sc Computer Science Programme shall be six semesters distributed over a period of three academic years. The odd semesters (1, 3, 5) shall be from June to October and the even Semesters(2, 4, 6) shall be from November to March. Each semester shall have 90 working days, inclusive of all examinations.The minimum duration for completion of a four semester PG Programme is two years and the maximum period for completion is 4 years. The duration of each semester shall be 90 working days, inclusive of examinations,spread over five months.Odd semesters shall be held from June to October and even semesters from inception and in the year 2011, it was rechristened as ‘Srishti’ with the caption, ‘Capture the Future’. A national Seminar entitled,‘Research Trends in computer science’, was also organized annually and was aimed to introduced the emerging areas to prospective researchers. The department offers Certificate course for Computer Professionals for UG and Certificate course in MATLAB for PG students.
